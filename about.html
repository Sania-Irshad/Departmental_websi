Introduction

The Department of Mathematics (CASPAM) at BZU Multan provides quality education in pure and applied mathematics. The department focuses on academic excellence and research-based learning.

Vision

  • To become a leading mathematics department in Pakistan.
  • To promote research and innovation.
  • To develop analytical thinkers.

Mission

    Provide quality education in mathematics. Encourage research culture. Prepare students for professional careers.

Chairperson Message

"Mathematics is the foundation of science and technology. Our department is committed to empowering students with strong analytical and research skills."

Dr. Imran Javed (Director of CASPAM)
